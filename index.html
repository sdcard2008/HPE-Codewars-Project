Tips on how to stay safe on the internet

Some tips for staying safe on the internet:

Use a VPN : VPN sounds kind of sophisticated , but it really isn't. This piece of technology changes your IP adress , thus hiding your location even after some kind of attack happens.
Choosing a strong password: Normally when you register in a website , google recommends you a strong password. ENABLE THAT. This will make password cracking much harder.
Dont react to scam calls and phishing mails: Scam calls are easily identified if you understand what they are saying you to do.
